## Appendix A: GST Invoice Requirements (For Reference)

As per Indian GST law, a tax invoice must contain:
1. Name, address, and GSTIN of the supplier
2. Invoice number and date
3. Name, address, and GSTIN of the recipient (if registered)
4. HSN/SAC codes for goods/services
5. Quantity and unit of goods
6. Taxable value and discounts
7. Rate and amount of CGST, SGST, IGST, or cess
8. Place of supply and state code
9. Signature of the supplier

**Note**: This App helps generate invoices with these details. Ensure all information is accurate before issuing invoices.
